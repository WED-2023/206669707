About Me

My name is Gili Gilat. I am a devoted mother of four and proud grandmother of three, with another little one on the way.

I am currently employed at Hitachi and am also nurturing a family business on the side, specializing in homemade jams. I reside in Ramot HaShavim.

My interests include reading, solving puzzles and Sudoku, traveling, and spending quality time with my family. I am looking forward to an upcoming late retirement, which I plan to fill with more family time and adventures around the globe.
